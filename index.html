Experience Downtown Toronto

Overview

Experience the pulse of downtown Toronto at the Toronto Marriott City Centre Hotel. Situated in one of Canada’s most vibrant districts, our hotel offers direct access to attractions like the CN Tower, Rogers Centre, and Ripley’s Aquarium. With modern amenities and expansive rooms, whether you're here for business or leisure, every detail is crafted meticulously for an unparalleled experience.

Our hotel is designed to seamlessly blend luxury with convenience—ideal for corporate meetings, memorable getaways, or exciting sporting events.

Meetings & Events

Our versatile meeting spaces and event venues are tailored for every occasion—from intimate gatherings to large conferences. With state‑of‑the‑art technology, customizable catering, and sophisticated settings, we ensure your event is executed flawlessly.

Dining

Indulge in a variety of culinary delights at our on‑site restaurant or savor your favorite coffee at our in‑house Starbucks®. Our dining options are curated to satisfy the most discerning palates with an exceptional gastronomic experience.
