Overview

Experience the pulse of downtown Toronto at the Toronto Marriott City Centre Hotel. Situated in one of Canada’s most vibrant districts, our hotel offers guests direct access to top attractions like the CN Tower, Rogers Centre, and Ripley’s Aquarium. With modern amenities and expansive rooms, you can enjoy business, leisure, and sports events in equal measure.

Whether you’re here for a corporate meeting, a memorable getaway, or an exciting sporting event, our hotel is meticulously designed to combine luxury with convenience.
